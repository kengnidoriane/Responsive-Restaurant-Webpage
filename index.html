About Us

About Restaurant

Nestled in the pulsating heart of Manhattan, Bubble reimagines Franco-American gastronomy through a lens of artistic audacity. Our glass-walled culinary theater overlooks Times Square, where Michelin-starred chefs fuse Parisian techniques with New York’s multicultural essence—think miso-cured foie gras paired with Harlem honeycomb, or deconstructed bagel montages served on recycled Broadway marquee tiles. Powered by a zero-emission kitchen and rooftop hydroponic garden, we transform locally foraged ingredients (Hudson Valley truffles, Brooklyn rooftop basil) into edible installations that challenge conventional dining paradigms.

Born from a 2005 underground supper club beneath Grand Central Terminal, Bubble gained cult status through our "Subway Series" menus served in repurposed MetroCards. The 2015 acquisition of a disused Broadway spotlight system birthed our signature "Dish Illumination" concept, while our 2023 partnership with MoMA codified food as performance art. Milestones include the 2020 World’s First Carbon-Negative Tasting Menu and our ongoing "Concrete Jungle Foraging" program training homeless New Yorkers in urban agriculture—a tribute to the city that shaped us.
